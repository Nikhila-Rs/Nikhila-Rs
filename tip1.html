Tip 1: Reduce, Reuse, Recycle

The 3 Rs—Reduce, Reuse, and Recycle—are fundamental to minimizing waste and protecting the environment:

  • Reduce: Avoid unnecessary consumption and waste by buying only what you need.
  • Reuse: Find new uses for old items rather than discarding them.
  • Recycle: Properly sort and dispose of recyclables to minimize landfill waste.
